Upcoming Changes

Sprucemarks has been available for free since 2011. The free model was great to start with, but it limits the amount of features, improvements, and support I can offer.

That's why Sprucemarks will become Patreon supported software as early as January 1, 2020. Your Patreon support will allow me to work on the following:

  • Refactor with modern JavaScript.
  • Better sorting for multilingual characters.
  • New extension name, graphics, and options screen.
  • User customizable logic for custom sorting rules.
  • Possible Firefox compatibility.
  • Support tools, documentation, videos, and more.

Once the Patreon changes are live, you'll be able to access Sprucemarks for as little as $1 a month, or unlock Sprucemarks ongoing once you donate $7 or more.

For anyone not interested in becoming a Patreon, you may want to make a copy of the current version of Sprucemarks before January 1, 2020. Use the current version for personal use or even as the basis of your own extension. Just make sure to not use the word "Sprucemarks" or any of the images included as they are strictly copyrighted to Kai Nightmode.

That's it and thanks for reading. If you have any questions, feel free to message me on Twitter. ^_^
